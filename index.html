The Story Behind This

She weighed 118kg, could barely breathe, and was running out of time.

Her lungs were compressed under the weight. Her doctor said she could stop breathing in her sleep — and that surgery was the only answer. But she wasn't ready to give up on herself first.

— A real patient, 60 years old. This app was built for her.

She couldn't run. She couldn't do yoga. She couldn't even lie flat without her body working against her. The conventional advice — "eat less, move more" — felt like a cruel joke from people who had never been in her body.

What she could do was breathe deliberately for 10 minutes. She could switch from juice to water. She could sleep at an angle instead of flat. She could write down what she ate. Small, unglamorous things that no fitness influencer would ever post about.

HealthPath was designed around what she could actually do — not what an ideal patient could do. Every feature, every checklist item, every motivational message comes from the real plan built for her real situation.

If it works for someone who can barely breathe, it works for you too.

The Journey

Progress happens
week by week — not overnight

Week 1
The Invisible Work
Request CPAP. Sleep at an angle. Cut liquid calories. Write down what you eat. None of this looks dramatic. All of it matters enormously.
Week 2
Building the Habit Stack
Morning and night breathing exercises. Protein at breakfast. The body starts to notice. Energy slightly improves. Sleep gets a little better.
Week 3
The First Real Changes
Protein at all three meals. Smaller portions. Seated exercises. The chart starts to curve downward. The streak counter grows. Something shifts psychologically.
Week 4 and Beyond
Momentum Becomes the Motivator
10-minute daily walks. Supplements. A 1-month review. By now, the habits aren't hard anymore — they're just what you do. The motivation problem has solved itself.
